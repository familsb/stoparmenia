#STOP HIDING HISTORY

Countless attrocities committed by the Armenian government against ethnic Azerbaijanis have gone unnoticed by the international community.

Khojaly Massacre

February 25-26, 1992

Khojaly, between Khankendi and Askeran, was the second largest Azerbaijani settlement in Nagorno-Karabakh. On the night of 25 to 26 February 1992, the Armenian armed forces attacked Khojaly with the direct participation of the 366th Motorized Rifle Military Regiment placed by the Soviet Union in Khankendi. Artillery and heavy military equipment fired on Khojaly, severe fires broke out in the city and it was completely engulfed in flames and destroyed.

I

The investigation revealed that the Armenian armed forces raided the town of Khojaly at about 10:00 PM, first fired heavy artillery, then entered the city with numerous tanks, PMDs and armored personal carriers, terrified the sleeping civilians in their homes and tortured and killed them savagely. Women, children and elderly were shot and murdered mercilessly. City locals were forced to leave their houses and the city. Khojaly was occupied until 05:00 AM on February 26th.
